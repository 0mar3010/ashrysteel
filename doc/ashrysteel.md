## 2. THEMATIC SYNTHESIS (cross-source, de-duplicated)

### Company History
- Egyptian, privately-held, family-linked steel group led by Chairman **Ayman El Ashry**. Founded either **1980** (AISU/legacy text only) or **1983** (LinkedIn + majority of business press and directories — see conflict log). Grew from a rebar/sections producer into a multi-factory, multi-company group via organic expansion and at least one major acquisition (Arcosteel, ~2022).
- Headquartered in **Zamalek, Cairo** (5 Mohamed Thaqeb/Sakeb St), with production facilities in **Qalyubia** (est. 2010), **6th of October City** (rebar mill + newer Galfa lighting-poles/galvanizing complex), **10th of Ramadan** (industrial/distribution arm), and an expanding footprint in **Sadat City** (metals-forming complex, planned wire and wheel-manufacturing plants). Arcosteel operates from **Sadat City** as the group's special/pre-stressed steel arm.

### Products
- **Long products:** reinforcing steel (rebar) 10–40mm including new **B420 DWR seismic-resistant high-strength grade**; equal angles up to 50×6mm; flat bars up to 50×10mm; merchant bars/sections.
- **Tubular/flat products:** ERW (electric-resistance-welded) longitudinally welded pipes ½"–12"; cut-to-length and slit steel coil/sheet; corrugated iron sheet; "beam Sigal."
- **Fabricated/engineered products:** lighting poles (street, highway, plaza, stadium — up to 30m, largest single volume item, 15,000+ units supplied to government projects), structural/transmission towers (low & medium voltage), communication towers, cable trays, scaffolding, greenhouse steel structures (pipe frames and fittings for agricultural projects), fencing components.
- **Special steel (via Arcosteel):** pre-stressed and stranded wire for railway sleepers; columns and coils for automotive, gearbox, excavator, and pump industries; wire for springs, nails, galvanized wire.
- **Services:** galvanizing (Middle East's largest single galvanizing line, 10,000 tons/month), cutting/slitting, project consultation through installation, maintenance services.
